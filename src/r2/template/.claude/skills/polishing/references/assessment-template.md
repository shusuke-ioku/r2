## Category Sets by Assessor

### Proofreader categories
- Forward reference
- Expectation violation
- Logical gap
- Undefined term
- Buried claim
- Redundancy (not strategic restatement)
- Pacing problem
- Dangling thread
- Section form deviation (reader experience)

### Calibration Assessor categories
- Verb calibration (F1/F3: verb does not match research design)
- Hedge asymmetry (F2: uniform hedging or main finding hedged)
- Limitation placement (F4: standalone section or excessive words)
- Topic sentence (F5: literature summary opener where claim needed)
- Intro timing (F6: finding deferred past paragraph 3)
- Conclusion overrun (F7: too long or introduces new analysis)
- Restatement gap (F8: main finding missing from expected location)
- Paragraph bloat (F9: results >7 sentences, body >10)
- Voice passivity (F10: passive construction on core finding)
- Assertiveness failure (CW attributed to "scholars," apologetic framing)
- Citation practice (dump >4 without differentiation, decorative)
- Word-budget gap (section >30% below or above benchmark)
- Overclaiming (verb exceeds design support)
- Underclaiming / too-conceding (hedging where assertion warranted)

### Humanizer categories
- Significance inflation
- Superficial -ing phrase
- Copula avoidance
- Rule of three
- Negative parallelism
- Synonym cycling
- Em-dash overuse
- AI vocabulary
- Sentence uniformity
- Soulless register
